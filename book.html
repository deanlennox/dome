Seasonal Guide

Summer (Dec - Feb)

Warm to hot days, perfect for dam swims. Late golden sunsets and warm, starry nights for the outdoor bath.

Autumn (Mar - May)

The winelands turn vibrant amber and gold. Mild days and crisp, fresh evenings. The best time for trail hiking.

Winter (Jun - Aug)

Cool, rainy, and incredibly cozy. The farm landscape turns a lush, vibrant green. Perfect for a glass of red by the fire.

Spring (Sep - Nov)

Wildflowers in bloom and newborn foals on the farm. Fresh breezes and mild temperatures. Nature at its most alive.
