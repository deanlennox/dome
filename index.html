About the Dome

Designed for
connection

Escape to nature at Protea Dome — a handcrafted, eco-friendly retreat perched above a tranquil dam on Rivendale Winelands Riding Club farm. Overlooking the sweeping Paarl/Stellenbosch valley, the geodesic dome offers a sustainable and serene getaway, finished with 100% linen bedding and an outdoor bath made for stargazing.

Designed for couples or small families seeking simplicity, comfort, and a genuine connection to the natural world.

Handcrafted geodesic dome
Outdoor stargazing bath
100% natural linen bedding
Eco-friendly design
Dam & valley views

What to do
while you're here

All activities are offered at Rivendale Winelands Riding Club and must be booked in conjunction with your stay, subject to availability.

01
On horseback
Horse Riding
Guided rides through the farm's trails with views of the dam and valley. Beginners and experienced riders welcome.
02
Creative
Art Classes
Guided sessions in the serene farm environment. Ideal for all levels — draw inspiration from the textures and colours around you.
03
Sustainability
Clay Brick Building
Learn the ancient craft of natural clay brick making — a hands-on workshop rooted in sustainable, low-impact building.
Getting Here

Close to everything,
far from it all

Situated on Tinkerberg Road in the Paarl Farms valley, Protea Dome sits at the doorstep of the Cape Winelands — conveniently near Stellenbosch, Franschhoek and the wine route, yet completely removed from city noise.

📍
Address Tinkerberg Road, Paarl Farms, Cape Town, 7620
🚗
Distance ~45 min from Cape Town CBD · 20 min from Stellenbosch · 25 min from Paarl
🌿
Nearby Wine estates, hiking trails, Paarl Mountain Nature Reserve
